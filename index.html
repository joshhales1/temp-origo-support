Privacy Policy for Origo

Effective Date: 06/06/24

Last Updated: 06/06/24

Thank you for using Origo ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app, which integrates with Strava data upon user consent. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information Collected from Strava:

Upon your consent, we collect the following data from your Strava account:

  • Activity Data: This includes your activity details such as type, duration, distance, and other metrics recorded by Strava.
  • Profile Information: This includes your Strava profile information such as your name, profile picture, and other public information on your Strava profile.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected from Strava for the following purposes:

  • Personalization: To provide you with personalized insights and recommendations based on your Strava activities.
  • Performance Tracking: To track and display your activity performance over time.
  • Feature Improvement: To improve our app’s features and functionality based on user activities and preferences.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data Storage: We do not store any information collected from your device. All data retrieved from Strava is processed in real-time and used solely for providing app functionality.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption and secure server protocols.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, or trade your Strava data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you have given us explicit consent to do so.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your Strava data permissions through the Strava settings.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time by disconnecting your Strava account from our app.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data from our systems by contacting us at [Your Contact Information].

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party’s site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
